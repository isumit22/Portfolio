My Journey as a Front-End Developer

I’ll be honest – when I first started learning to code, it felt like I was trying to solve a puzzle with no picture on the box. HTML and CSS were a bit confusing, and JavaScript? Well, that seemed like another language entirely. But over time, things started clicking.
Now, I love bringing ideas to life on the web, whether it’s through a simple webpage or a more complex interactive design. Every project teaches me something new, and I’m always finding new ways to make things look good and work better. Plus, I have to admit – ChatGPT is my secret weapon for coding help. It’s like having a friendly coding companion who always has a solution (or at least a suggestion) ready.
I’m still early in my dev journey, but I’m always learning and growing. For me, coding is all about creating cool, user-friendly experiences. I’m excited for what’s next, and I can’t wait to see where this path takes me!
